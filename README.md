# Fearless
## I am John Jolly, and I am Fearless.

### I am a husband.
I love my wife. I am proud to be with this farmers daughter. I honor her choice to stay by my side through joy and sorrow, fortune and famine. I respect her right to choose what she does, who she loves, how she acts, and where she wants to go. I may not agree with her all the time, but I always trust her to choose what is good. 

I love my wife fearlessly.

I respect the decision of a person to be with the companion of their choice. I will trust people to make the best choice for them. I may not agree with that choice, but I recognize it is not my choice to make. I expect the same level of acceptance for my choice of companion. you may not agree with my choice, but I trust you will allow me to make my choice. 

I do not fear your choice.

### I am a father.

### I am a programmer.

### I am a Christian.

### I am an American.
